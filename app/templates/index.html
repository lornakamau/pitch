What is a pitch?

A pitch is a presentation of a business idea to a potential investor, cutomer or just a third party

Types of pitches


Twitter Pitch

This summarizes your whole idea in one punchy sentence and is used to gauge your audience's receptiveness. This is best for when you meet someone at an event and they have never heard of your company.

View examples

Elevator Pitch

The idea is that this pitch should last as long as an elevator ride, that is between 15 seconds and a couple of minutes. Should be clear, concise, definitive, engaging and brief.
